<h3>How to delete your account.</h3>

1. Login to the app
2. Go to settings (click the gear icon in the lower right)
3. Click "Account Management" to open the account management menu
4. Click "Delete Account"

Note: This will permanently delete your profile and messages from our servers,
there is currently no grace period! Profile information and messages may be
retained for a limited time if your account has been reported by another user
to help us investigate.

For more information, see the 
<a href="/dpp">Data Privacy Policy</a>.
